Refunds

Donations made through Scholastic Challenge are non-refundable, except as specified below. In all cases, including those listed below, no refunds can be issued after 30 days beyond the date of the event.

  • The donation was made in error and you notify Scholastic Challenge of the error within 24 hours of the time of the donation.
  • An error was made during the processing of your donation that caused your credit card to be charged incorrectly. This would include your card inadvertently being charged twice.

    • To request a refund, please contact us at support@@scholasticchallenge.com
